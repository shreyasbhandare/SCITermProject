About Me ...

Currently I am pursuing master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rutgers University - New Brunswick, where my concentration is Computer Engineering and Networking.

Prior to this, I have completed my bachelor's degree in Instrumentation engineering from University of Mumbai. Instrumentation is a field which incorporates knowledge from all over the engineering streams. This background makes me flexible to learn everything in technology, and when you do that, you gain whole around aspects of it and provide best solutions. Honestly, it's the best thing!

Technical negotiations and delivering best technology solutions is what I learned from my experience of roughly around 18 months.

Right now, I am working as an IT Support Specialist in Rutgers Business School - Office of Technology and Instructional Services. My job here is to configure and build new systems and perform system administration tasks for Windows and Linux based systems.
